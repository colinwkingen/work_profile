Stuff about me.

I have lived my entire life in Portland Oregon, and if I ever leave I'll surely return. Right now my wife Kara and I live on the North side, near St. Johns. Besides coding and web work I love to cook, bike, read and experiment with electronic music and abstract painting. I can sometimes be found in dingy old man bars, parks with tall trees and restaurants that serve shellfish.

I have experience with:

  • Python 3
  • HTML and CSS
  • Bootstrap 3
  • Javascript
  • Git

These are relatively new skills for me, but I am committed to learning more about them every day and becoming a better coder and designer. I am currently enrolled at Epicodus, a coding school for web developers to accelerate this process.
